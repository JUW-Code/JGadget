Support Center

Shipping Policy

We offer worldwide shipping on all our premium gadgets.

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days.
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days.
  • Overnight: Available for select locations.

Once your order is placed, you will receive a tracking number via email within 24 hours.

Return & Refund Policy

Important: Please read our refund terms carefully.

We stand by the quality of our products. However, if you need to return an item, please note the following:

  • Returns must be initiated within 14 days of receipt.
  • Items must be in original, unopened condition.
  • Refund Amount: Only 50% of the purchase amount will be refunded for all returns, as per our restocking and processing fee policy.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

When will I receive my preorder?

Preorders are prioritized and will ship on the official release date. You will be notified via email.

Do you offer warranty?

Yes, all devices come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and bank transfers.
